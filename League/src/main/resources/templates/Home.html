When was MYA created?

MYA was created in the 1992 in South Minneapolis MYA(which stands for Minnesota Youth Association) was founded by John Doe John Doe created mya because he wanted the surrounding youth to be better than him.

Minnesota has been founded by many org and wealthy people throughout its lifespan and whenever there's a tragic event in our community we are one of the first people there because if we don't want to help our community who will?
